How to play?

Step into the kitchen chaos! Grab ingredients from the crates, chop them if needed, and deliver them to the serving counter. Every correct dish earns points, but mistakes will cost you—so work fast and stay sharp! Beat the clock, rack up the highest score, and climb the leaderboard to claim your spot at the top!
